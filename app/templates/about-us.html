Our Story & Mission

Our company was founded with a passion for crafting heartfelt ceremonies that unite families and celebrate life’s milestones. Driven by values of inclusivity, tradition, and genuine care, we design celebrations that honor your unique journey.

Every ceremony is a blend of time-honored rituals and personal touches, ensuring that each event is as unique as the family it celebrates.

Meet the Team

Celia

Celia is a celebrated celebrant who customizes ceremonies for every occasion. With a background in fashion and culinary arts, a seminary degree, and a celebrant certification, she crafts bespoke celebrations that reflect your personality, preferences, and stories. Celia seamlessly blends tradition with modern creativity—and if you fancy a delicious chocolate mousse recipe, she’s got that secret too.

Michael

Michael is a compassionate officiant dedicated to adapting ceremonies to your vision. With a warm and professional approach, he artfully combines cherished traditions with personalized elements and selects distinctive readings to make your ceremony both memorable and meaningful. And if you need help untangling a tricky calculus problem, Michael is always ready to lend a hand.

Cari

Cari brings the enchanting magic of musical theatre to your ceremony. With experience from Disney Cruise Lines and a proud membership in Actor’s Equity, her vibrant presence and passionate, melodic voice infuse every event with sincerity and fun. With Cari, you can expect a symphony of heartfelt moments—plus a musical tip perfectly suited to your theme.
